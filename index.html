About the Creator

One lesson can become a legacy.

Tony Ferlazzo created the Driver Confidence Guide to help people become more comfortable, capable, and confident with cars. Along the way, he discovered a larger idea: everyone has something worth teaching.

The Driver Confidence Guide is the first Learning Adventure built around that idea.
