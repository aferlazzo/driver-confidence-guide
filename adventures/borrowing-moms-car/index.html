Episode 1

Borrow Mom's Car

Every car is different. Two minutes of preparation can save a lot of confusion.

Decision 1 of 2

Mom sets the keys on the counter.

Mom: “You can borrow my car. Bring it back with some gas in it.”

Tyler is already waiting outside.

Tyler: “Road trip. How different can one car be from another?”

Alex sits behind the wheel. The dashboard, controls, and phone connection are all unfamiliar.

What should Alex do first?

Take two minutes to locate the main controls and owner's manual. Start driving and figure everything out along the way. Ask Mom about anything that looks unfamiliar. Assume every car works exactly the same.
