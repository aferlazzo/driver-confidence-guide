Episode 1

Borrow Mom's Car

Decision 1 of 2

Alex has permission to borrow his mom's car for the afternoon. Maya is waiting outside, and they are already running a few minutes late.

Mom: “The keys are on the counter. Please bring the car back with some gas in it.”

Alex gets into the driver's seat and realizes this car's dashboard and controls are different from the car he usually drives.

What should Alex do first?

Look around the car and locate the owner's manual. Start driving and figure everything out along the way. Ask where the manual is before leaving. Assume every car works exactly the same.
