Version 2 Design Principles
The experience comes first.

Visitors should immediately understand what they can do.

Stories first.

Readers come for the adventures.

They stay because they enjoy the characters.

Missions support stories.

Missions are reference material.

They exist to help readers solve problems during adventures.

Every page asks the reader to do something.

Never present a dead end.

Always invite the next action.

Less reading.

More comics.

More choices.

More interaction.

Every episode should answer one question.

"What happens next?"

Every episode ends with a teaser.

Give readers a reason to come back.

Humor is required.

Tyler's job is to keep things from becoming a lecture.

Characters grow.

Alex becomes a better driver.

Readers grow with Alex.

Learning should feel accidental.

Readers come for the story.

They leave knowing something useful.
