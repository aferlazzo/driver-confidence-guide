# Version 2 Vision

## Purpose

Version 2 transforms the Driver Confidence Guide from a digital handbook into an interactive comic series built on the Adventure Learning method.

The goal is to help people become safer, smarter drivers through entertaining stories, practical choices, and reusable learning missions.

## Product Structure

### Adventures are the primary experience

Readers choose realistic activities such as:

- Borrow Mom's Car
- Go to the Zoo
- Go Camping
- Movie Night
- Drive Home at Night
- Arizona Monsoon

Each adventure contains:

- Recurring characters
- Comic story panels
- Realistic decisions
- Humor
- Links to relevant missions
- A clear return to the story
- A satisfying ending

### Missions support the stories

Missions teach one practical skill at a time.

They remain reusable reference material and may appear in many different adventures.

The missions are not the main product. They are the learning library that helps characters and readers solve problems.

## Version 2 Priorities

1. Replace the long homepage introduction with a short invitation.
2. Make adventures the most prominent part of the site.
3. Add Comic Life 3 panels to adventure scenes.
4. Complete Season 1.
5. Improve mobile readability.
6. Add analytics for:
   - Adventure started
   - Mission opened
   - Return to story
   - Adventure completed
7. Keep the tone useful, human, and funny.
8. Test the experience with real readers.

## Design Rule

Every feature must support this question:

> Does this make the interactive comic series more useful, enjoyable, or memorable?

If the answer is no, do not build it.

## Development Rule

Version 1 remains frozen.

All experiments and redesign work happen only on the `version2` branch.

Version 2 is not published to the live site until it has been reviewed and tested.
